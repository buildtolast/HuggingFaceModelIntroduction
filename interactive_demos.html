6. Training Methods Simulation

Compare different training approaches: Full fine-tuning, LoRA, and QLoRA

Training Method Comparison

Method Trainable Params VRAM (7B) Training Time Quality Best For

Training Flow Example (QLoRA)

# 1. Load base model (quantized) model = load_quantized_model("meta-llama/Llama-2-7b") # 2. Add LoRA adapters (1.6M params) lora_config = LoraConfig(r=8, lora_alpha=16, target_modules=["q_proj", "v_proj"]) # 3. Setup training trainer = SFTTrainer( model=model, train_dataset=dataset, peft_config=lora_config, max_seq_length=512, epochs=3, per_device_train_batch_size=4, ) # 4. Train trainer.train() # 5. Save adapters only (light weight) model.save_pretrained("my-finetuned-model") # ~50MB instead of 14GB!

Why QLoRA is Popular in 2026

✅ Advantages

• 75% VRAM savings

• Works on RTX 4090 (24GB)

• Fast training (3-8 hours)

• Adapters are portable

⚠️ Trade-offs

• Slightly slower training

• Quality ≈ 95% of full FT

• Limited architectural changes
